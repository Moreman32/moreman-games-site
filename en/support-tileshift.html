Support — TileShift

TileShift is a game by Moreman Games. If you need help, want to report a bug, or would like to submit a privacy-related request, contact us at:

Support email: support@moreman.games

Typical response time: within 3–5 business days.

What we can help with

  • launch issues, freezes, or crashes;
  • progress, UI, or localization problems;
  • questions about ads, purchases, or app behavior;
  • privacy-related requests and data deletion requests.

What to include in your email

  • game title: TileShift;
  • device model and OS version;
  • a short description of the issue;
  • if possible, a screenshot or steps to reproduce the problem.

Data deletion request

If you want to request deletion of data related to your use of the game, email support@moreman.games with the subject Data Deletion Request.

Helpful details: include the game title, platform, device, and a short description of the request. This helps us respond faster and process your request correctly.
