TileShift

Welcome to TileShift — a unique game where puzzles, storytelling, and visual delight merge into one vivid adventure! Together with the cute cat Busya, you will:

  • Collect tiles across stylish levels with carefully tuned balance and increasing difficulty
  • Complete more than 200 diverse levels
  • Follow the story presented as interactive comics — every 20 levels brings a new chapter
  • Explore different eras and adventures — from modern Cyprus to futuristic worlds
  • Enjoy a detailed world map that evolves every 200 levels
  • Relax with atmospheric music and sounds that create comfort and immersion
  • Unlock comics and tiles, and watch Busya’s story unfold

TileShift is more than just a puzzle game. It is a visual adventure filled with soul, humor, and captivating storytelling. Every tap, every match, and every level is a step into the wonderful dreamworld of a cute cat.
