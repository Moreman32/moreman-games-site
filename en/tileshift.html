TileShift: Businka Mahjong

TileShift: Businka Mahjong is a relaxing tile-matching puzzle game inspired by classic mahjong mechanics and cozy casual adventures.

Join Businka the cat on a calm and colorful journey through handcrafted levels, beautiful locations, and satisfying puzzles.

Features

  • Relaxing and intuitive gameplay
  • More than 800 handcrafted levels
  • Cozy cat-themed atmosphere
  • Beautiful colorful visuals
  • Smooth animations and satisfying effects
  • Helpful boosters and power-ups
  • Daily rewards and progression
  • Designed for short relaxing sessions or long playtime

Match tiles, clear the board, and sharpen your attention and strategy skills while enjoying a warm and cozy visual style.

Unlock new challenges, discover charming locations, and follow Businka through relaxing puzzle adventures.

Whether you want to unwind after work or challenge yourself with increasingly difficult puzzles, TileShift: Businka Mahjong is easy to learn and satisfying to master.
