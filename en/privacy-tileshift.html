Privacy Policy — TileShift

Effective Date: August 15, 2025
Last Updated: August 15, 2025

TileShift is a mobile game developed by Moreman Games. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting player data. This Privacy Policy describes what information may be collected, how it is used, and what rights you have.

1. What data we collect

We do not collect personal information (such as name, address, or email) unless you provide it voluntarily (for example, when contacting support). The game and integrated SDKs may collect anonymized technical information, such as:

  • Device information: model, OS, language, region, IP (anonymized)
  • Unique identifiers: IDFA (iOS), AAID (Android), Firebase ID
  • Gameplay data: progress, time spent, ad interactions
  • Diagnostics: crashes, errors, logs
  • Advertising data: impressions, clicks, anti-fraud protection

2. Purpose of data use

  • Running the app and saving progress
  • Gameplay analytics and quality improvements
  • Displaying and personalizing ads (with your consent)
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • App updates and bug fixing

3. Third-party services and SDKs

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Unity Ads — advertising and analytics (privacy policy)
  • Firebase Analytics — analytics (privacy policy)
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting
  • GameAnalytics — gameplay analytics (privacy policy)
  • Appsflyer (if used) — attribution (privacy policy)

4. Data transfer and storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Anonymized data may be transferred to third-party servers (EU, US, and others). We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Legal basis

  • GDPR/UK GDPR: Legitimate Interest (analytics, security), Consent (ads)
  • CCPA/CPRA: We do not sell personal data; you may opt out of data sharing
  • COPPA: The game is not directed at children under 13

6. User rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct or delete your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@moreman.games

7. User controls

  • Reset IDFA/AAID through device settings
  • Disable personalized ads via iOS/Android settings
  • Limit analytics through in-app or device settings

8. Security

We use secure transmission (HTTPS) and trusted SDKs. We do not request or process sensitive personal information.

9. Data retention

Technical and analytics data are stored only as long as necessary for the stated purposes. Local game data is deleted automatically when the app is uninstalled.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The effective date will be updated at the top of the document.

11. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: support@moreman.games
