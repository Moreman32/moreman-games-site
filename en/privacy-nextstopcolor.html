Privacy Policy — Next Stop: Color!

Next Stop: Color! is a mobile game by Moreman Games. We respect your privacy and collect only the minimum information required to run and improve the game.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect personal data (such as name or email) without your consent. Third‑party SDKs may collect anonymized technical data via the game engine.

  • Device type and OS
  • Region and language
  • Gameplay activity and events

2. Ads and analytics

We may use third‑party services (e.g., Unity Ads, AdMob, Firebase Analytics). They can collect anonymized data to personalize ads and analyze usage.

Learn more: Unity Privacy Policy

3. Your choices

You can limit ad tracking at the system level (IDFA/AAID settings). You can also contact us to remove any information we control.
