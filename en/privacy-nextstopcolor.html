Privacy Policy — Next Stop: Color!

Next Stop: Color! is a mobile game developed by Moreman Games. We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring the security of your information.

1. What data is collected

We do not collect personal data (name, email, phone, etc.). The game may collect anonymized technical data through Unity’s built-in tools.

  • Device type and OS
  • Language and country
  • Sessions, engagement level, and in-game actions

2. Advertising and analytics

The game uses Unity Ads — Unity’s advertising platform. Unity may use anonymized data for advertising and analytics purposes.

More details: Unity Privacy Policy.

3. Data storage

All progress and settings data are stored locally on your device. We do not transfer data to third parties.

4. Security

We use only trusted technologies to ensure safe app usage. Personal data is not requested or processed.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@moreman.games
