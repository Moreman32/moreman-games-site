Privacy Policy — Next Stop: Color!

Effective date: August 15, 2025
Last update: August 15, 2025

Next Stop: Color! is a mobile game developed by Moreman Games. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy describes what data may be collected, how it is used, and what rights you have.

1. What data is collected

We do not request personal information (such as name, email, phone, etc.) unless you provide it voluntarily (e.g., when contacting support). The game and integrated SDKs may automatically collect anonymized technical data:

  • Device and app information: device model, OS version, app version, language, region, IP (anonymized), timezone.
  • Identifiers: advertising ID (IDFA on iOS / AAID on Android), Firebase Instance ID.
  • Usage and events: screen opens, session length, level progress, clicks, ad views, install source; crashes and diagnostic data.
  • Approximate location: inferred from IP (city/country) — for antifraud, analytics, and ad targeting.

2. Purpose of data use

  • Ensuring correct app functionality and saving game progress.
  • Analyzing usage and improving game quality and balance.
  • Displaying and personalizing ads (with user consent).
  • Fraud prevention, security, and crash diagnostics.
  • Compliance with platform (Google/Apple) and legal requirements.

3. SDKs and third-party services

We may use third-party services (the set may vary by version/build):

  • Unity Ads — advertising display, frequency capping, antifraud (policy).
  • Firebase Analytics — usage analytics (policy).
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports and diagnostic data.
  • GameAnalytics (if used) — gameplay analytics (policy).
  • Appsflyer (if used) — campaign attribution (policy).

Some providers/SDKs may act as independent data controllers with respect to part of the collected data and process it under their own privacy policies.

4. Data storage and transfer

  • Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Anonymized technical data may be transferred to partner servers (EU/US and other regions).
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Legal basis for processing

  • GDPR/UK GDPR: legitimate interests (analytics, security, product improvement) and consent (personalized ads/ad measurement).
  • CCPA/CPRA: we do not sell personal data; you may opt out of “sharing” for targeted advertising.
  • COPPA: the game is not intended for children under 13; we do not knowingly collect children’s data.

6. User rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • access, correction, or deletion of data;
  • restriction of processing, objection to processing;
  • data portability (if applicable);
  • withdrawal of consent for personalized advertising.

Requests should be sent to: support@moreman.games.

7. Settings and controls

  • Reset advertising ID IDFA/AAID — in device settings.
  • Disable personalized ads — in iOS/Android settings or in the consent window (if displayed).
  • Limit analytics collection — in app/device settings (depends on platform and SDK version).

8. Security

We use secure data transfer (HTTPS) and trusted SDKs; however, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Data retention

Technical and analytics data are stored only as long as necessary for the stated purposes, after which they are aggregated or deleted. Local game data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. See the effective date at the top of the document. In case of significant changes, we may notify users via the app or website.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries and to exercise your rights, contact: support@moreman.games
